1. Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500 g chicken (cut into serving pieces)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger (sliced)
  • 2 potatoes (cubed)
  • 1 carrot (sliced)
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced, optional)
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk (or 1 small can)
  • 1‚Äì1¬Ω cups water
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pot. Saut√© garlic, onion, and ginger.
  2. Add chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Sprinkle curry powder and mix well to coat the chicken.
  4. Add water and bring to a boil.
  5. Add potatoes and carrots. Cover and simmer until chicken and veggies are tender.
  6. Pour in coconut milk and bell pepper.
  7. Simmer (do not heavily boil) for a few minutes until sauce thickens.
  8. Season with salt and pepper.
  9. Serve hot with rice.
