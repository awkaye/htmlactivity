1. Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp melted butter or oil
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk egg, milk, and melted butter/oil.
  3. Pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients and mix gently (okay if a bit lumpy).
  4. Heat a lightly oiled pan on medium heat.
  5. Pour a small amount of batter (about ¬º cup) into the pan.
  6. Cook until bubbles appear on top, then flip and cook the other side until golden.
  7. Serve with syrup, butter, or fruits.
