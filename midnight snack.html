1. French Fries

Ingredients:

  • 2â€“3 potatoes
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt
  • Optional: cheese powder, sour and cream powder, or barbeque powder

Steps:

  1. Peel potatoes (optional) and cut into thin sticks.
  2. Rinse in water, then pat dry with a towel.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan.
  4. Fry potatoes in batches until golden and crispy.
  5. Remove and drain on paper towel.
  6. Sprinkle salt (and other seasonings if you like).
  7. Serve hot.
