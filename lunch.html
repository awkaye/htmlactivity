1. Tortang Repolyo (Cabbage Omelette)

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 small carrot (grated, optional)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp flour or cornstarch
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2‚Äì3 tbsp oil

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, combine cabbage, carrot, and onion.
  2. Add eggs and mix well.
  3. Add flour/cornstarch, salt, and pepper. Mix until coated.
  4. Heat oil in a pan.
  5. Pour a portion of the mixture and flatten into a round patty.
  6. Fry on medium heat until golden brown on one side, then flip and cook the other side.
  7. Repeat with the rest of the mixture.
  8. Serve with rice and ketchup or soy sauce.
